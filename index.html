How to get Spotify credentials

Follow these steps to create a free Spotify developer app.

1
Go to developer.spotify.com/dashboard
2
Click "Create App"
3
Fill in:
App name: N96_freq
Description: My personal music player
Redirect URI: http://127.0.0.1:3001/api/spotify/callback
Check: Web API
4
Click "Save"
5
Copy the "Client ID" and paste it into the wizard
6
Click "Show Client Secret" → Copy → Paste into the wizard
7
Done!
